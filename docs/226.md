<p>Invert a binary tree.</p>

<p><strong>Example:</strong></p>

<p>Input:</p>

<pre>
     4
   /   \
  2     7
 / \   / \
1   3 6   9</pre>

<pre>
     4
   /   \
  7     2
 / \   / \
9   6 3   1</pre>
